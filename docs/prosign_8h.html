#define M8 (   d)    (B8(d##1) << (CHAR_BIT * (sizeof MorseToken(0)) - sizeof #d))

Generate a binary literal with upto 7 bits in a 16-bit field left justified and terminated with a 1 as a sentinel. To serialize the bits from left to right, extract the left-most bit by masking against MORSE_MSB and left shift one place.
